# Goal

We are building and maintaining cookiecutter-django-rest, a Cookiecutter template that scaffolds modern, production-ready Django REST Framework APIs. The next milestone is completing the modernization of the template to Python 3.13+, Django 5.0+, and PostgreSQL 16.4+, ensuring all generated project dependencies, CI workflows, and Docker configs reflect current best practices. Daemon should focus on working through the open modernization and maintenance specs under `.daemon/specs/`, resolving outdated dependencies, updating CI configuration, and ensuring the generated scaffold is clean, tested, and fully functional out of the box.
